# cateval: Validation of Conditional Average Treatment Effects

Tools for validating the calibration of predicted treatment benefit from
treatment-selection models with a continuous outcome. The package covers
the two calibration analyses used in the validation procedure: overall
benefit, based on matched concordant and discordant patients, and
pairwise drug-class effects. Benefit is summarised by group (calibration
plot), by the mean benefit of following the model, and by the
calibration-in-the-large and calibration slope, each with analytical or
optional pair-level bootstrap confidence intervals.
